History

Since 1996, TechCon North America has been produced by TJ|H2b
Analytical Services, a senior leader in the oil analysis industry.

Mission

The primary goal of TechCon is to foster the advancement of technology
and innovation for the betterment of humanity. Provide a platform for
the exchange of ideas and the exploration of emerging technologies.
Facilitate collaboration and networking among industry leaders,
entrepreneurs, and researchers
